ABOUT ME

 ARTIST. CARTOONIST. DESIGNER

Hello! My name is Carlos Burgess. I'm an artist located in Virginia Beach, VA. I've basically been drawing all my life. I haven't done any exhibitions or showcased my work at any art galleries (too shy I guess). However, I'm setting up this site to show you some of my art work and my cartoon strips to which I'm having a lot of fun working on. I don't have much formal art training because I was too busy serving in the Army, and working as a technician at the CDC when I was living in Atlanta. I'm also currently dealing with chronic kidney failure to which can be a drag. However, you can check out more of my cartoon side of me on my instagram page by clicking the Carlos Cartoon Universe link below. Thanks for coming to my site and enjoy!
